About

Based in Colorado, I'm a full stack web developer, explorer, and former teacher. My programming background started in high school where I learned how to create animations and simple games like blackjack. I'm passionate about learning new technologies to improve the overall experience and maintainability of websites.

When I'm not crafting a new project or solving coding puzzles, you'll find me adventuring, soaking up nature, and enjoying time with friends and family. Whether it's a quick hike nearby or in another country, I'm always up to explore new things.

My objective is to craft delightfully practical things with great people.
